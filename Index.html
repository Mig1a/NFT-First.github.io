What is an NFT?

NFT stands for non fungible token, a digital asset that is not mutually interchangeable,which comes with a proof of ownership and authenticity.An NFT can include art, music, in-game items, videos, and other media. They are purchased and sold online, frequently using cryptocurrency, and are typically encoded with the same underlying software as many cryptos.

Some prominent examples in use today are:

  • A unique piece of digital artwork. Much like a unique piece of art from your favorite painter, NFTs are in use for digital artwork. Each unique digital image has its own ID, which the owner possesses.

  • A ticket that gives you access to an event.- Not all NFTs are currently being used to make big art purchases. Because of their one-of-a-kind ownership, NFTs are ideal assets for things like sporting events. Your ticket is unique to you, making copying or stealing impossible.
