What is Mint?

The process of converting a digital file into a crypto collectible is known as NFT minting. It is impossible to delete, edit, or modify a blockchain-stored digital item or file. "Minting" refers to the process of uploading an item to the blockchain, which is similar to the creation of fiat coins. The Ethereum blockchain is used for the majority of NFT minting because it is the first choice for most digital art and digital content creators and artists.

The first thing you need to do is purchase Ethereum.There are several exchanges to buy ETH from,but the easiest,most user friendly option is coin base

Once you purchased Ethereum you need to set an ERC compatible crypto wallet. I suggest Metamask ,it is the best and most popular option. After you set up your Metamask account,you are ready to send ethereum to it.

Now you are ready to purchase an NFT. Visit an NFT marketplace, such as Opensea and connect your wallet to purchase your first NFT.
